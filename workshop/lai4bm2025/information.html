Pre-arrival Information

Workshop Venue:
HKUST Jockey Club Institute for Advanced Study
Lo Ka Chung Building, Lee Shau Kee Campus
The Hong Kong University of Science & Technology
Clear Water Bay, Kowloon, Hong Kong, China

Getting to HKUST:

  • From Hong Kong International Airport: Take Airport Express to Kowloon Station, then taxi to HKUST (approximately 45 minutes total)
  • By MTR: Take MTR to Choi Hung Station, then take bus 91M to HKUST
  • By Taxi: Direct taxi from any location in Hong Kong (show driver: 香港科技大學)
  • By Bus: Various bus routes serve HKUST including 91M, 792M, and others

Campus Maps:
Campus maps and detailed directions are available in the docs folder. Please refer to the location maps provided for detailed campus navigation.

Important Notes:

  • Please bring a valid photo ID for campus entry
  • The workshop will be held in English
  • Free WiFi is available throughout the campus
  • Parking is available on campus (charges apply)
