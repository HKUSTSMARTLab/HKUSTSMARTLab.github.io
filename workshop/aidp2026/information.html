VISA Requirements

Important: You are strongly recommended to check visa requirements from the Hong Kong Immigration Department website before planning your visit. Holders of passports from most developed countries normally do not require a visa to enter Hong Kong. Please allow sufficient time for visa applications prior to your travel.

Weather

Hong Kong has a sub-tropical climate with distinct seasons. Typhoon season begins in May and ends in November. When a typhoon is approaching, warnings are broadcast on television and radio.

November Weather

Temperature (Day): Usually around 24°C
Temperature (Night): Usually around 19°C
Humidity: Moderate
Conditions: Mild and generally pleasant, with cooler evenings and occasional rain

Typhoon Information

When typhoon signal no. 8 is in place, most businesses and shops close down and flights may be cancelled. There is a separate warning system for heavy rain.

Payment Methods

Credit Cards

American Express, VISA, and MasterCard are widely accepted at hotels, shops, and restaurants.

Mobile Payments

Apple Pay, Google Pay, Alipay, and WeChat Pay are accepted at many stores. Look for payment signs at entrances.

Octopus Card

Essential for public transport and many shops. You can purchase a physical card or download the tourist app.

Learn more about Octopus

ATM Services

ATMs are widely available throughout Hong Kong. Many accept international cards, and HSBC machines provide 24-hour cash withdrawal services for Visa and MasterCard holders. Some ATMs also offer foreign currency withdrawal.
